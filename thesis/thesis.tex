\chapter{Introduction}

The increasing complexity of software inevitably increases number of bugs
found in it. A bug-free software is a myth but there are many ways to prevent
this from happening? One of them is abstract interpretation.

This thesis describes how abstract interpretation works

Chapter~\ref{chap:preliminaries} presents preliminaries required to understand
the abstract interpretation. In chapter~\ref{chap:design} several string
abstract domains are designed. The implementation of designed domains is
described in great detail in chapter~\ref{chap:implementation}. Finally,
thesis findings are summarized in chapter~\ref{chap:conclusion}.
